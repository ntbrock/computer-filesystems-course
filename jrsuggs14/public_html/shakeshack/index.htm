 My Favorite Species is Rainbow Trout because of how smart they are and how much of a fight they put up. Most fish will take the biggest meal they can get and are not easily spooked from clumsy fishing. Rainbow trout, on the other hand, are very cautious and will only go with food found normally in the particular river that they are in and are always cautious. If they spot, feel, or even smell that something is off, then they won’t bite at all
The three most popular types of fishing
Bait FishingFly FishingTrolling
Bait Fishing is anything from using spinning reels and artificial lures to actual live bait. It is the most popular type of fishing. The main points of bait fishing is that you cast the weight of the lure out and the reel is able to free spin and independently release fishing line. Fly Fishing is not as popular as bait fishing, but it does have its own positives. Instead of casting the weight of the lure, you are casting the weight of the fly line. The reel doesn’t freespin so casting requires manual unravelling of the line. The benefits to fly fishing is that the gear is much lighter and delicate, meaning the fish with the fish is more difficult. Trolling, while popular, is considered to be the slowest form of fishing. Trolling involves putting out baits on long length of fishing line in order to be pulled behind a boat. Although you can cover a lot of water quickly, trolling ends up being just a wait because there is no need to cast or move to a different fishing spot manually.
